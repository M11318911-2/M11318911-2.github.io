NVIDIA GTC 2026 揭幕：Vera Rubin 平台正式登場，Feynman 架構預覽
中文

NVIDIA 執行長黃仁勳在 GTC 2026 主題演講中慶祝 CUDA 20 週年，並發表全新 Vera Rubin 平台。該平台整合七顆晶片、五套機架級系統與一台超級電腦，專為代理式 AI 打造。黃仁勳指出，過去幾年運算需求增長百萬倍，NVIDIA 預計 2025 至 2027 年間將創造 1 兆美元營收。大會同時預覽未來架構 Feynman 與 Rosa CPU。

English

NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang opened GTC 2026 with a keynote celebrating 20 years of CUDA and unveiling the all-new Vera Rubin platform — a full-stack computing platform with seven chips, five rack-scale systems, and one supercomputer, purpose-built for agentic AI. Huang noted computing demand has surged a million-fold, with NVIDIA projecting $1 trillion in revenue from 2025 through 2027. The conference also previewed the future Feynman architecture and the Rosa CPU.

Mistral 獲 8.3 億美元融資，歐洲 AI 自立拉響號角
中文

法國 AI 新創 Mistral 宣布完成 8.3 億美元債務融資，將採購 1.38 萬顆 NVIDIA 晶片，在巴黎近郊興建大型資料中心。Mistral 計劃在 2027 年底前於歐洲部署 200 百萬瓦算力，並擴展至瑞典。這是歐洲想在 AI 領域建立自主供應鏈的最明確訊號。

English

French AI startup Mistral announced an $830 million debt financing deal to purchase 13,800 NVIDIA chips for a major data center near Paris. Mistral plans to deploy 200 megawatts of compute across Europe by end of 2027, with expansion into Sweden. This is one of the clearest signals yet that Europe intends to build its own AI supply chain, from models to compute.

中文

English

Chinese AI lab DeepSeek's chatbot service suffered a 7-hour and 13-minute outage on March 30th — its longest disruption since its breakout in early 2025. Known for rapid iteration, the incident raises questions about its operational infrastructure. Analysts say AI labs are transitioning from pure performance competition to needing enterprise-grade reliability as they scale.
