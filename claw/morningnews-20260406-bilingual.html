OpenAI 收購科技播客 TBPN 首度跨足媒體業

來源: TechCrunch
中文

OpenAI 宣布收購熱門科技產業播客節目 TBPN（Technology Business Programming Network），這是這家 AI 巨頭首次收購媒體公司。TBPN 由前科技創業家約翰·庫桑和喬迪·海斯主持，是一個每日在 YouTube 和 X 平台直播三小時的節目，專注於科技、商業、AI 和國防議題。該節目在矽谷擁有忠實觀眾，包括馬克·祖伯格、薩蒂亞·納德拉、馬克·貝尼奧夫和 Sam Altman 等科技CEO都曾上過節目。根據《華爾街日報》報導，TBPN 今年的收入預計將超過3000萬美元。OpenAI 表示 TBPN 將保持編輯獨立性，繼續自行選擇節目內容和嘉賓。

English

OpenAI has acquired popular tech industry talk show TBPN — Technology Business Programming Network — marking the AI giant's first acquisition of a media company. Hosted by former tech founders John Coogan and Jordi Hays, TBPN is a daily live show airing on YouTube and X for three hours, focusing on tech, business, AI, and defense. The show has gained a cult following in Silicon Valley, with top tech CEOs like Mark Zuckerberg, Satya Nadella, Marc Benioff, and Sam Altman appearing as guests. According to The Wall Street Journal, TBPN is on track to pull in more than $30 million this year. OpenAI says TBPN will maintain editorial independence and continue to choose its own programming and guests.

美國參議員調查自動駕駛公司對遠端輔助操作的透明度

來源: TechCrunch
中文

美國麻薩諸塞州民主黨參議員艾德·馬基向七家自動駕駛技術公司發出質詢信函，包括 Aurora、May Mobility、Motional、Nuro、Tesla、Waymo 和 Zoox，要求說明其車輛依賴遠端工作人員協助的頻率。然而，所有公司都拒絕透露相關數據。馬基參議員批評這是自動駕駛公司在遠端輔助操作員使用方面「令人震驚的缺乏透明度」。他已呼籲美國國家公路交通安全管理局展開調查，並表示正在研擬立法以對自動駕駛公司的遠端操作員使用施加嚴格限制。

English

Senator Ed Markey (D-MA) sent letters to seven U.S. companies — Aurora, May Mobility, Motional, Nuro, Tesla, Waymo, and Zoox — working on autonomous vehicle technology, asking how often their vehicles relied on input from remote staff. All companies refused to disclose the information. Senator Markey called it a "stunning lack of transparency from AV companies around their use of remote assistance operators." He has called on the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration to investigate and says he is working on legislation to impose strict guardrails on AV companies' use of remote operators.

Microsoft 宣布在日本投資 100 億美元擴展 AI 與網路防衛

來源: Bloomberg
中文

Microsoft 宣布將在日本投資 100 億美元，用於擴展 AI 基礎設施和網路防衛能力。這筆巨額投資是科技公司在亞洲地區最大規模的擴張行動之一，旨在強化日本在人工智慧和網路安全領域的實力。投資將包括資料中心建設、AI 研發中心的設立，以及與日本企業和政府在網路防衛方面的合作。此舉被視為美國科技巨頭在美日同盟框架下，深化兩國科技合作的重要舉措。

English

Microsoft announced it will invest $10 billion in Japan to expand AI infrastructure and cyber defense capabilities. This massive investment is one of the largest tech expansions in Asia, aiming to strengthen Japan's capabilities in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. The investment will include building data centers, establishing AI research and development centers, and collaborating with Japanese businesses and the government on cyber defense. This move is seen as a significant step in deepening U.S.-Japan technology cooperation under the alliance framework.

Anthropic Claude 訂閱將不再涵蓋第三方工具使用

來源: LLM Stats
中文

Anthropic 宣布從4月4日下午12點（太平洋標準時間）開始，Claude 訂閱將不再涵蓋在第三方工具上的使用費用。此項政策變更旨在更好地管理容量資源。這項決定影響了許多依賴 Claude API 的開發者和企業用戶，他們需要重新評估其使用第三方工具的成本結構。專家指出，這反映了 AI 公司在資源分配和成本控制方面面臨的挑戰。

English

Anthropic announced that starting April 4 at 12pm PT, Claude subscriptions will no longer cover usage on third-party tools. This policy change aims to better manage capacity. The decision affects developers and enterprise users who rely on Claude API for third-party tool integrations, requiring them to reassess their cost structures. Experts note this reflects the challenges AI companies face in resource allocation and cost control.

Meta 執行長馬克·祖伯格重返程式編寫崗位

來源: LLM Stats
中文

根據《The Pragmatic Engineer》報導，Meta 執行長馬克·祖伯格在睽違二十年後重新回到程式編寫工作。他已向 Meta 的單一代碼庫提交了三個程式變更，並成為 Claude Code CLI 的重度使用者。這項消息在科技業界引發熱議，被視為一位科技巨頭執行長親身體驗 AI 程式開發工具的指標性案例。祖伯格的親自參與可能為 Meta 未來的 AI 開發策略帶來新的方向。

English

According to The Pragmatic Engineer, Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg is back to writing code after a two-decade hiatus, submitting three diffs to Meta's monorepo and becoming a heavy user of Claude Code CLI. This news has sparked discussion in the tech industry, seen as a landmark case of a tech giant CEO personally experiencing AI-powered software development tools. Zuckerberg's personal involvement could signal new directions for Meta's future AI development strategy.
